About Me

I am a Junior Front End Developer seeking for new challenges.

In the past few years I've been a medical student, I joined an international board of experts in a project held by the European Brain Council and I worked as a remote office assistant.

Last year, I decided to give to my life a new twist and I enrolled in the online program offered by Udacity to learn Frontend development.

As I went through the program, I felt powerful when I saw the first red "Hello World!" appeared on my screen after I wrote a couple of lines in HTML and CSS; slightly scared when I met JavaScript and things got serious and then deeply in love when I started hanging out with React. I was amazed by realizing how much work there is behind even the apparently simplest landing page and how much the user must be kept in mind in terms of design, accessibility and responsiveness of the product.

Whenever I'm not coding, I spend my time reading, binge-watching Netflix Tv-series or trying new things. Recently, I try out climbing (definitely not my thing), squash (I love it!) and now I'm all about learning to paint. However, my most adored and money-consuming passion is and always will be travelling.

Please, have a look at my Linkedin profile or my GitHub repos and, if you feel like we could be a match, feel free to contact me for a get-to-know chat, a coffee or the life-changing offer, I'm ready for all of them!
